Enter chords and lyrics, one per line in the format: "Chord - Lyrics"

Supported chord types:

  • Basic chords: C, Am, F#m
  • Extended chords: Cmaj7, Dm7, G7
  • Slash chords: C/G, Am/F, Fmaj7/C

Example:
C - Verse start
Am/F - With bass note
Fmaj7 - Extended chord
G7/B - Combined

You can also enter just a chord name to see its visualisation.
